# 📊 Northwind Traders Business Insights & Sales Analytics

## 📖 Overview

This project analyzes the Northwind Traders dataset to extract meaningful business insights using SQL and Power BI. The objective is to understand customer behavior, sales trends, product performance, and operational efficiency through exploratory data analysis and interactive dashboards.

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* SQL (MySQL / SQL Server)
* Power BI
* Excel

## 📊 Key Analysis

* Customer segmentation and high-value customer identification
* Sales and revenue trend analysis over time
* Order volume, value distribution, and shipping performance
* Product and category-wise sales analysis
* Supplier contribution and pricing analysis
* Employee distribution and performance insights

## 📈 Dashboard Features

* Interactive Power BI dashboard with multiple views
* KPI cards (Total Revenue, Orders, Customers, Products)
* Sales trend analysis (monthly/yearly)
* Customer distribution using map visualization
* Product and category performance visuals
* Shipping and delivery performance tracking

## 💡 Key Insights

* A small group of customers contributes significantly to total revenue
* Sales show seasonal trends and fluctuations over time
* Top-performing products drive a major share of revenue
* Most orders are small, while bulk orders contribute heavily to sales
* Certain regions dominate overall sales performance
* Discounts increase order quantity but may reduce profit margins

## 🚀 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analytics can transform raw business data into actionable insights. By analyzing customer behavior, sales patterns, and operational metrics, businesses can improve decision-making, optimize performance, and drive growth.
